Joby was selected for the White House-backed eVTOL Integration Pilot Program, enabling early operations across 10 U.S. states (Arizona, Florida, Idaho, New Jersey, New York, North Carolina, Oklahoma, Oregon, Texas, and Utah) beginning in 2026 ahead of FAA type certification. The program includes Joby's autonomous Superpilot technology, with flights expected within 90 days of contract finalization and production scaling to four aircraft per month in 2027.
