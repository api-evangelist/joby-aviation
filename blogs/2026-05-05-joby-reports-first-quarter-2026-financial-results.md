Joby reported Q1 2026 results, ending the quarter with $2.5 billion in cash, cash equivalents and short-term investments. The company grew composites production to more than 2.5 times last year's volume, expanded its Ohio footprint to nearly 1.5 million sqft, completed first FAA-conforming aircraft flights for Type Inspection Authorization, passed the SR3 audit (third of four major FAA certification reviews), and flew a 148-mile turbine-electric VTOL flight at maximum take-off weight.
