Joby details the environmental case for its all-electric air taxis, noting a route from Century Plaza to John Wayne Airport could use 34% less energy per passenger-mile than a gasoline car, rising to a 47% emissions reduction on Southern California's cleaner grid. The company says renewed energy procurement has avoided over 5,100 metric tons of greenhouse gas emissions, and it reached its lowest-ever emissions intensity by revenue in 2025 with 80% of company electricity from renewables.
