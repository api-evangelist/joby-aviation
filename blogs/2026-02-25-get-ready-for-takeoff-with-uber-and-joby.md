Joby and Uber introduced Uber Air powered by Joby, letting riders book electric air taxi service directly through the Uber app with one-tap booking and multi-modal ground-plus-air trips. Joby's aircraft carry up to four passengers at speeds up to 200 mph with a 100-mile range, and the company expects to carry passengers later in 2026 in Dubai, building on collaboration since 2019.
