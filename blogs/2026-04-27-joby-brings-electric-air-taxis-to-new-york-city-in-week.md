Joby completed New York City's first-ever eVTOL air taxi demonstration flights, connecting JFK to Manhattan heliports in under 10 minutes during a week-long campaign under the federal eVTOL Integration Pilot Program. Founder and CEO JoeBen Bevirt framed the flights as showing "what the next chapter looks like: a quiet, zero operating emissions air taxi service designed to better serve New Yorkers."
