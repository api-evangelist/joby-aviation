Joby partnered with Air Space Intelligence to integrate electric air taxi operations into the U.S. National Airspace System using ASI's AI-powered Flyways platform. The companies expect to conduct live operational exercises later in 2026 with results anticipated by year-end, aligning with Joby's role in the White House-backed eVTOL Integration Pilot Program enabling early operations across 12 states.
