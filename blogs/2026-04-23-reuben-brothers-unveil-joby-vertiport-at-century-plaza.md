Reuben Brothers partnered with Joby to establish an air taxi vertiport at Park Elm Residences at Century Plaza in Los Angeles, using the building's existing helipad for operations and charging of Joby's eVTOL aircraft. Billed as the first residential development in LA to offer in-house air taxi service, it will include Joby's first passenger lounge in a residential building, drawing on its nine lounges in New York and France.
