Joby completed piloted electric air taxi flights across San Francisco Bay and around the Golden Gate Bridge using aircraft N545JX flown by Andrea Pingitore from Oakland International Airport, kicking off its 2026 Electric Skies Tour. The company says it has logged more than 50,000 miles in test flights and plans to scale production to four aircraft monthly by 2027 across facilities in Ohio, Marina, and San Carlos.
